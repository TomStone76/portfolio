About Me

Welcome to my home on the Internet! I am glad you've decided to visit my site.

My name is Thomas Joseph Stone, and I am a Front-End Web Developer based in Philadelphia. As a developer, I view coding as not just a science, but an art form as well. For this reason, my philosophy emphasizes a harmonious balance of form and function. This means that I am passionate about creating sites and applications that are cleanly designed and aesthetically pleasing as well as functional.

My technical skills include HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript (including JQuery and Node.js), SQL (including MySQL), Firebase, the command line, and git.

Feel free to look at my portfolio and contact me with any questions.
